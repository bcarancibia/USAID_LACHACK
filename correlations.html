Honduras Correlations

One of the more interesting aspects of the data from Honduras is that there is a strong correlation between criminals deported from the United States to Honduras and the Honduras Homicide Rate. This means for the increase in criminals deported back to Honduras there is also a rise in the homicide rate within Honduras.
