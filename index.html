Security in Honduras

One of the key concerns and needs in Honduras is relible information on safety around the country. This is true even for the USAID Honduras Mission. One of the questions that the USAID Mission had trouble answering because of the lack of data is, where are our projects location and how dangerous are the areas that USAID officials and project implementers travel to to complete projects.

This is a quick implementation of different datasets to begin the process of informing users about different aspects of Honduras. To the left users can navigate to the Security Map, which shows homicide rate per municipio, US Citzen deaths in Honduras, and USAID Projects locations. A correlation graphic showing that US Deportations of Criminals coorelates with the Homicide Rate in Honduras, and a list of all datasets used.
